Importing

You can import your locally stored TV Shows and Movies by simply clicking the button. A Windows file select dialog will popup and you will be prompt to select your file. You can either input your personal metadeta, or if you'd like, you could input the TMDB ID, and we'll do it for you.

Scrobbling (Trakt.tv)

Trakt.tv is one of my favorite applications. I use it to track and collect all of my viewing activity. Rather than having to input all of your activity into Trakt.tv yourself, you can input your credentials and Fleets will do it for you.
